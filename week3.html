Story: Asking the Girl

(click/hover for ASL)

A: What's up?

B: Look at that girl. Do you like her?
Is she your friend?

A: No. Ask for her name.

B: I wonder if she will watch a movie with me.

A: I don't think so.

B: Why?

A: I want to ask her too.

B: Oh I see.

A: Let me ask her. You're awkward.

B: Please. I'm better.

A: Really? I want to see you be brave.

B: I'll say the best line. Watch me.

A: Ok.

...

B: Hello. How are you?

C: Good! Nice to meet you.

A: WHAT'S UP?

B: LOOK THAT GIRL. YOU LIKE HER?
SHE YOUR FRIEND?

A: NO. ASK NAME.

B: I WONDER IF SHE WATCH MOVIE WITH ME.

A: I NOT THINK SO.

B: WHY?

A: I WANT ASK TOO.

B: OH I SEE.

A: LET ME ASK. YOU AWKWARD.

B: PLEASE. I BETTER.

A: REALLY? I WANT SEE YOU BRAVE.

B: I SAY BEST SENTENCE. WATCH ME.

A: O-K.
